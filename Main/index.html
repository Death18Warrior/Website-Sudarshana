Overview

The tragic incidents of the Nirbhaya case and the Kolkata rape case, where women were subjected to brutal violence, served as a powerful catalyst for us to create a solution aimed at preventing such atrocities.In most cases, the victims suffered immense trauma, and the lack of timely intervention highlighted the vulnerability women face in public spaces. These events deeply affected us and inspired the development of Sudarshana, a wearable safety device designed to offer real-time monitoring and automatic distress alerts.Sudarshana is built to detect signs of panic or danger, triggering alerts with the user’s exact GPS location to pre-selected emergency contacts.The device aims to empower women by providing an immediate, discreet way to call for help, even when they are unable to do so manually. Our mission is to give women the power to feel safe anywhere, with an automated, real-time alert system that ensures help is always one step ahead, no matter what.

How it Works?

Sudarshana is your invisible guardian—a smart wearable that monitors your vitals and movements, detecting distress in real-time. Without the need for buttons or delays, it instantly alerts your trusted contacts with your exact location when you need help the most.Whether you're at home or on the go, Sudarshana ensures your safety, offering peace of mind anytime, anywhere. It’s designed to keep you protected, providing swift assistance when every second counts.

Features

1. Smart Panic Detection: Continuous monitoring with machine learning for accurate distress detection.
2. Personalized Profiles: Custom user profiles to minimize false alerts by recognizing stress and adrenaline.
3. Geofencing Alerts: Proactive notifications when entering unsafe zones or risk areas.
4. Emergency Response: Instant alerts to pre-configured contacts with real-time location during emergencies.
5. Advanced Sensors: Integrated sensors for precise monitoring and detection.

Impact

Sudarshana addresses critical gaps in women’s safety by offering a smart wearable that detects distress through abnormal heart rates or sudden movements and automatically alerts emergency contacts. Unlike traditional measures requiring manual intervention, Sudarshana ensures timely assistance even when women are incapacitated by fear or restraint. By aligning with initiatives like the Nirbhaya Fund, Women Helpline (181) and the Safe City Project, and reinforcing laws such as the Criminal Law (Amendment) Act (2013) and the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (2005), it provides an advanced, reliable and discreet safety solution.
