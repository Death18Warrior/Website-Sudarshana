Overview

The tragic incidents of the Nirbhaya and Kolkata rape cases highlighted the urgent need for better personal safety solutions. These events inspired us to create Sudarshana, a wearable device designed to protect women in real time. Sudarshana detects signs of distress and automatically sends alerts, including the user's GPS location, to pre-selected emergency contacts. It offers a discreet, hands-free way to call for help when manual intervention isnâ€™t possible. We are committed to leveraging technology to ensure women always have safety at their fingertips.

How it Works?

Sudarshana is a smart wearable designed for real-time protection.It continuously monitors vitals and movements, detecting distress without manual activation. If danger is detected, it instantly alerts trusted contacts with the exact location, ensuring help arrives when it matters most. Whether at home or on the move, Sudarshana provides seamless protection and a peace of mind, keeping safety always within reach.

Features

1. Real-Time Distress Detection: AI-powered monitoring for accurate distress detection.
2. Personalized Profiles: Adapts to individual patterns to reduce false alerts.
3. Geofencing Alerts: Sends warnings when entering high-risk areas.
4. Emergency Response: Instant location-based alerts to trusted contacts.
5. Advanced Sensors: High-precision sensors for real-time safety monitoring.

Impact

Sudarshana bridges gaps in women's safety by offering a smart wearable that detects distress through abnormal heart rates or sudden movements, automatically alerting emergency contacts. Unlike traditional methods, which require manual intervention, Sudarshana ensures timely assistance even when fear or restraint prevents action. By aligning with initiatives like the Nirbhaya Fund, Women Helpline (181), and the Safe City Project, and supporting laws like the Criminal Law (Amendment) Act (2013) and the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (2005), Sudarshana offers a reliable, discreet, and advanced safety solution.
